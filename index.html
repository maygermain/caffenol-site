Caffenol - A homemade alternative to traditional black and white developer.

This site contains information on film development using caffenol, a homemade developer made of coffee, washing soda and vitamin C.

Information on caffenol development is scattered across a number of sites, and can be difficult to track down for beginners. We hope this site will help you understand the caffenol development process more simply!
